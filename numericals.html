1 · Contrast stretching Session 2

Map \([r_{\min}, r_{\max}] \to [0, L-1]\):   \(s = (r-r_{\min})\,\frac{L-1}{r_{\max}-r_{\min}}\).

[50,150] → [0,255], r = 100:   slope \(=255/100 = 2.55\);   \(s=(100-50)(2.55)=127.5\approx \mathbf{128}\).

3 · Gradient magnitude & direction Session 4

Clean vertical edge (Prewitt)

Patch dark-left/bright-right → \(g_x=(200-10)\times3 = 570,\ g_y=0\).

\(\|\nabla f\|=570,\ \theta=0°\) → vertical edge.

Class "0's and 2's" diagonal

\(g_x=-2,\ g_y=2\) → \(\|\nabla f\|=\sqrt8=2.83\).

\(\theta=\operatorname{atan2}(2,-2)=135°\); edge direction \(=45°\) (⊥).

4 · Hough collinearity test Session 5

Are \((1,2),(2,3),(3,4)\) collinear? Each → \(c=-x\,m+y\):

\(c=-m+2,\ \ c=-2m+3,\ \ c=-3m+4\). First two: \(-m+2=-2m+3\Rightarrow m=1,\ c=1\). Third checks: \(-3+4=1\) ✓.

Result

Collinear; common line \(y=x+1\) at \((m,c)=(1,1)\).

5 · Laplacian at an edge (5×5, edge between 150|50) Session 4

4-neighbour \(K_1\)

Bright-side pixel (=150): \(-4(150)+(150{+}150{+}150{+}50)=-600+500=\mathbf{-100}\).

Dark-side (=50): \(-4(50)+300=\mathbf{+100}\).

8-neighbour \(K_2\)

Bright-side: \(-8(150)+900=\mathbf{-300}\).

Dark-side: \(-8(50)+700=\mathbf{+300}\). Sign flip = zero-crossing at edge.

6 · Harris corner response Session 6

\(R=\det M - k(\text{trace }M)^2\), \(k=0.04\):

Corner

\(\sum I_x^2{=}2,\sum I_y^2{=}2,\sum I_xI_y{=}0\): \(\det{=}4,\text{tr}{=}4\), \(R=4-0.04(16)=\mathbf{3.36}>0\).

Edge

\(\sum I_x^2{=}2,\sum I_y^2{=}0\): \(\det{=}0,\text{tr}{=}2\), \(R=0-0.04(4)=\mathbf{-0.16}<0\).

7 · HOG histogram (class 5×5) Session 6

Central 3×3 gradients → magnitudes & angles → 9-bin histogram (simple binning):

Angles ≈ {36.87° ×3, 21.8°, 26.57°, 21.8°, 11.31°, 14.04°, 11.31°}, magnitudes ≈ {25×3, 26.9, 22.4, 26.9, 25.5, 20.6, 25.5}.

Bin 0–20° (angles ~11–14°): \(25.5+20.6+25.5 = 71.6\).   Bin 20–40° (angles ~22–37°): \(25{+}25{+}25{+}26.9{+}22.4{+}26.9 = 151.2\).

Histogram

\([71.6,\ 151.2,\ 0,0,0,0,0,0,0]\) → then L2-normalise.

8 · HOG interpolated binning, θ = 57° Session 6/7

9 bins of 20°, levels 0,20,…,160. 57° between 40° and 60°:

to 40°: \((60-57)/20=0.15\); to 60°: \((57-40)/20=0.85\). With \(|g|=85\): bin40 = 12.75, bin60 = 72.25.
